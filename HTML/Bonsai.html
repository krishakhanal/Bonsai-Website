    • Bonsai is a combination of Japanese techniques used to cultivate and style a beautiful tree with the goal of miniature it.
    Two Japanese characters planted in a small container make up the phrase "bonsai. Any kind of tree or shrub can be converted
    into a bonsai with the right care and styling techniques. A bonsai tree can easily reach the age of over 100.
    Making a realistic image of nature is the aim of bonsai cultivation. Over 2000 years ago, China is where the practice of preserving tiny trees began.
    About a thousand years ago, Japanese culture began cultivating bonsai trees.

    Garden Tools

    Pruning Shears

    These type of tools are to be bought first because when the tree grows long shoots
    it is required to trim them and it is the right
    tool to do so. It is useful than scissors because it can reach various areas which scissors cannot reach.
    It is very much useful for clean cut and it doesnot crush twings and shoots.

    Bolt Cutter

    It is also one of the valuable tool added in bonsai tool kit. Cutting
    blades of these tool are shaped likes two halves of a sphere, a shape which allows the knob cutter to bite into wood
    aggressively. It can remove a lot of wood quickly,
    even if it is large enough to shape the final cut surface on a trunk or branch.

    Sieve

    This tool is essential, especially when mixing and repotting your own soil. It is primarily used to sift through soil and is an excellent practice.
    Repotting is an important part of the growing process for bonsai plants.
    Sieves can be used to remove compact dust that causes drainage blockage in your bonsai pot.
