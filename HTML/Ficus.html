    Ficus


    The most widely used indoor species of trees for beginning Bonsai practitioners is the Ficus genus,
    which is a member of the Moraceae family of mulberry trees.
    They are highly ideal for indoor Bonsai and may be found in the tropics on every continent.
    These tropical-native bonsai are recognized by their glossy leaves and thick branches.
    Because they can be trained to grow in a variety of different styles and are generally easy to maintain, ficus bonsai are very popular.

    Bonsai Specifications:
    - Botanical Name: Ficus Microcarpa
    - Common Name: Ginseng Ficus
    - Size: 9" - 12" Including Growers Pot
