    Chinese Elm


    Chinese elm:Small, delicate leaves and appealing, textured bark are two characteristics of Chinese elm bonsai.
    They may be grown both inside and
    outside and require only a minimal amount of maintenance. South-east Asia and China are the original home
    of the Chinese Elm. It can grow to be a large tree up to 80 feet tall in its natural habitat (25 :meter).
    It becomes finely ramified with little leaves, which makes it an excellent Bonsai plant.

    Bonsai Specifications:
    - Age Range: 5-7 Years Old
    - Height: 10" - 14” Tall Potted
    - Classification: Broadleaf Semi Deciduous
